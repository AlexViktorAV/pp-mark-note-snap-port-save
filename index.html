Privacy Policy for MNCMP Highlighter

Effective Date: July 17, 2026

TL;DR: MNCMP Highlighter is a 100% local, privacy-first extension. We do not have servers, we do not track your browsing history, and your data never leaves your machine unless you explicitly export a backup file.

1. Data Collection & Usage

All highlights, custom note placements, coordinates, and configuration profiles are stored exclusively inside your browser using the local storage environment (chrome.storage.local). No external telemetry loops or server connections are registered.

2. Permissions Disclosures

  • storage: Required to save your annotations locally on your machine.
  • activeTab / scripting: Used exclusively to render localized highlighting marks, pointer SVGs, and overlays on the web pages you actively choose to interact with.
  • downloads: Used to fulfill your explicit requests to export local data packages, images, and backup files to your standard local downloads directory.
  • contextMenus: Enables right-click context paths to quickly highlight selections or drop sticky notes.

3. Third-Party Sharing

Since your information is strictly hosted inside your local Chrome profile configuration sandbox, your data is never sold, analyzed, or shared with third-party entities.

4. Contact & Support

For questions or extension inquiries, please open an issue directly on our GitHub project repository page.
